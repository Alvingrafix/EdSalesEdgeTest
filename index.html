Community Guidelines

Our core values are **Share Yes / Sell No**, the **Chatham House Rule**, and **Support Without Competition**. This is our manifesto.

Our Rituals & Rules

  • **Mon Momentum**: Share your weekly goals.
  • **Win Wed**: Celebrate your wins, big or small.
  • **Fri Ship**: What did you launch or ship this week?
  • **Welcome**: Greet new members.
  • **Graduation**: When you've outgrown us, celebrate it!

Who Edge is NOT for

This community is for ambitious edtech founders who are serious about sales and building their business. It is not for people looking to just "network" or find shortcuts without putting in the work.
